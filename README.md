🚀 ScaleUp
ScaleUp is an AI-powered social growth agent built for creators and small businesses who want clarity, not guesswork.

It analyzes public social media data — posts, engagement, audience reactions, and competitor performance — to turn raw signals into clear, actionable growth insights.

✨ Why ScaleUp?
Most creators and small businesses struggle with:

Posting consistently but not growing
Not knowing why competitors perform better
Relying on vanity metrics instead of real signals
Guessing what content might work next
ScaleUp fixes this by combining audience intelligence + competitive analysis to show what actually drives growth.

🔍 What ScaleUp Does
Analyzes recent posts and engagement patterns
Extracts meaningful insights from Publicly Avaliable data.
Detects sentiment, audience demand, and recurring pain points
Tracks competitor performance and content strategies
Identifies content gaps and underperforming areas
Suggests what to post, when to post, and in which format
All insights are designed to be clear, practical, and easy to act on.

⚙️ How It Works
Fetches recent posts and engagement data for the user and competitors
Normalizes posts, comments, and performance metrics
Filters high-signal comments based on engagement
Runs sentiment and theme analysis
Compares patterns across accounts and formats
Converts insights into clear growth recommendations
